Wibblymat's LD26 page

Ludum Dare is a 48 hour game jam held three times a year. Competitors from around the world will create games from scratch in just 48 hours based on a theme to be announced at the start of the event. Over a thousand games are expected to be created in just one weekend, and this page details the creation of mine.
